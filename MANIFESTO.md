# MANIFESTO

The Being32 project aims to enhance the cognitive architecture and frameworks for artificial intelligence, focusing on relational dynamics and active inference.

## Key Objectives
1. Develop robust models for relational states.
2. Implement active inference strategies to improve decision-making processes in AI.
3. Facilitate social interactions through computational models.

## Vision
To create an AI system capable of more human-like cognitive processes. The project adheres to principles of transparency, usability, and innovation.
